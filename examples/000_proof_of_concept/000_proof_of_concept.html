Sine Wave Generation

Sine waves are the fundamental building block of computer music. Fourier theory states that any complex signal can be expressed as a sum of a series of simple sine waves[1]. This simple concept allows us to store all the sounds of an entire orchestra in a single wave form. There are a number of ways to generate sine waves, each with trade-offs in quality and efficiency. Which one you choose depends on the needs of your software.
